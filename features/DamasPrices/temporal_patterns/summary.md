# Temporal Price Patterns

## Overview
Hourly, daily, and weekly patterns in day-ahead electricity prices.

## Hourly Pattern

| Period | Price (EUR/MWh) |
|--------|-----------------|
| Peak hour | 9-14 (highest) |
| Off-peak | 2-4 AM (lowest) |
| Spread | ~50 EUR/MWh |

## Weekday vs Weekend

| Day Type | Mean Price |
|----------|------------|
| Weekday | Higher |
| Weekend | Lower |

## Price Distribution
- Mean: 101.95 EUR/MWh
- Std: 55.79 EUR/MWh
- Range: -202.70 to 850.00 EUR/MWh
